Lisbon Macro Workshop

August 28–29, 2026


The fifth edition of the Lisbon Macro Workshop will be held on August 28–29, 2026, at Nova School of Business & Economics.

We are currently taking submissions and welcome all macro papers as well as expressions of interest in being a discussant. Submitting authors are expected to be the presenter. The final program will reflect the interests of the participants and organizers.

Submissions: you can make a submission with this form until March 29.

The workshop will cover travel and accommodation for discussants.

If you are interested, previous programs for the workshop are available here: 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025.

Any questions, please email us at lisbonmacro@gmail.com.
