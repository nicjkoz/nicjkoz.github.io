Lisbon Macro Workshop


The fourth edition of the Lisbon Macro Workshop will be held on August 29–30, 2025, at Católica Lisbon School of Business & Economics. We are currently taking submissions and welcome all macro papers as well as expressions of interest in being a discussant. The final program will reflect the interests of the participants and organizers.

Submissions: you can make a submission with this form until April 13.

Presenters will need to cover their own travel and accommodation. The workshop will cover these expenses for discussants.

If you are interested, previous programs for the workshop are available here: 2022, 2023, 2024

Any questions, please email us at lisbonmacro@gmail.com.

We thank Católica Lisbon School of Business & Economics, FCT and CEPR for supporting the workshop.

We look forward to seeing you in Lisbon!
